Nancy’s Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Muffins

2 eggs
1 Cup pumpkin
½ C melted butter
1 C chocolate chips
1 ½ C flour
1 C sugar
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. cinnamon
½ tsp. clove
¼ tsp. allspice
¼ tsp. salt  

1)    preheat oven 350 degrees.
2)    In large bowl, add eggs, pumpkin, butter, and chocolate chips.  Blend well.
3)    Add dry ingredients
4)    Spoon into lined muffin tins
5)    Bake 20-25 min.

Makes 1 dozen Muffins w/ tops… or 1 dozen flat muffins & 1 dozen mini muffins
